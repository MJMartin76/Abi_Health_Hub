Why This Diet Matters

For PCOS: Low glycemic foods prevent blood sugar spikes, balanced meals improve insulin sensitivity, regular meal timing helps regulate hormones.

For Gout: Low-purine foods prevent uric acid buildup, high water intake flushes uric acid, anti-inflammatory foods reduce joint pain.

No eggs: You're allergic - never eat eggs!

1 cup plain Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup mixed berries, 2 tbsp ground flaxseed, 1 tbsp honey, handful of almonds

ğŸš« Foods to AVOID

For Gout (High Purine):

  • Organ meats, red meat (beef, pork, lamb)
  • Certain seafood (anchovies, sardines, mackerel)
  • Bacon, sausage, hot dogs, alcohol

For PCOS (High Glycemic):

  • White bread, white rice, white pasta
  • Sugary drinks and sodas, candy, cookies, pastries
  • Processed foods and fast food
